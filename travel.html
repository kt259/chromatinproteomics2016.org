Travel

 

Getting to Heraklion

  • By air through Athens International Airport “Eleftherios Venizelos”:
    Services to Heraklion International Airport “Nikos Kazantzakis” are operated daily by Aegean Airlines and various regional flight operators. Flights from Athens [ATH] to Heraklion [HER], take approximately 45 min. Charter airlines operate direct chartered flights to Heraklion, daily from April to November, from major European cities.
    Heraklion airport: http://www.heraklion-airport.info/

  • By boat from Piraeus:
    Ferry services are operated daily by MINOAN LINES and ANEK SUPERFAST. Overnight services from Piraeus to Heraklion take approximately 10 hours.
    http://www.minoan.gr/en
    http://web.anek.gr/portal/page/portal/ANEK_prod

  • Local Transfers:
    In order to get to Aldemar Knossos Royal from Heraklion International Airport, “Nikos Kazantzakis” you have the following options:

    A) From the bus station outside the airport you can take the bus to Hersonissos. You get off at bus stop No 20 , there you can get a taxi to the hotel (the distance to the hotel is 3 km). The cost for the bus ticket is 3.30 euro. Schedule of buses from airport to Hersonissos: from Monday to Saturday from 6:00-8:00 every half an hour, from 8:00-21:45 every 15 minutes and then 22:15, 22:45,23:00,00:00 and 1:00am.

    B) At the airport bus station there is also bus to "Αnisaras (Aνισαράς) Hotels" that stops outside Aldemar Knossos Royal Village. It runs daily at 14:00, 15:45 ( mini bus ) and 21:00. The ticket costs 4 euros.

    C) In case you want to take a taxi from Heraklion airport to Aldemar Knossos Royal you can send an email to info@cretetaxi.com quoting our workshop, a taxi will be waiting for you at the airport arrivals. For all the participants of this Workshop there is a special fare price of 33 euros including VAT. Please have a look at their website: www.cretetaxi.com ( From the taxi station outside the airport the normal price is around 40 euros ). The distance is 23 km.

    If you want to go from the hotel to Heraklion city center, there is a daily bus departing from the main entrance of the hotel at 9:15 (ticket 4 euros). This bus passes by the airport (it takes half an hour).

  • Visa requirements:
    Citizens of the European Union or holders of a valid Schengen Visa do not need a visa to visit Greece.
    Citizens of all other countries should contact their nearest Greek Embassy or Consulate in order to obtain information regarding a visa to visit Greece.
    The organizers will provide any required documents confirming participation to the Scientific meeting only after registration is confirmed by fee payment.
